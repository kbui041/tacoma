Insurance Information

Tacoma Family Eyecare makes every effort to keep our eye care services easily accessible and affordable for our patients and their families.

Costco Members Special Pricing

Tacoma Family Eyecare is pleased to offer our Costco Members a combined routine vision service at a low warehouse price.

Invoiced as S0620 & S0621, these combined routine services are not billable to or reimbursed by Medicare, Medicaid, or Insurance.

Open-Access Providers

We are open-access providers for the following insurance plans. While we are not contracted directly with these insurers, we can still provide you with services and help you utilize your benefits.

How Open-Access Works:

  1. You pay for services directly to our office at the time of visit
  2. We provide you with an itemized receipt
  3. You self-submit the receipt to your insurance for reimbursement
  4. Your insurance reimburses you directly in 3-4 weeks according to your plan rules
VSP (including Uniform Medical Plan, Regence Blue Shield, City of Tacoma, Starbucks, Alaska Airlines, Amazon)
Davis Vision
Eyemed
Metlife
Molina
BlueView
Superior Vision
Cigna
Humana

VSP Patients:

Along with your itemized receipt, we will provide you with the VSP reimbursement form and an addressed envelope to VSP for easy self-submission.

Costco Optical Partnership:

Costco Optical is contracted with VSP, Davis Vision, and some Eyemed plans, so they will bill your glasses/contact lens purchases directly for you.
