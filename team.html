Our Professional Team

At Tacoma Family Eyecare, our team of experienced optometrists and staff members are dedicated to providing exceptional care. We combine clinical expertise with genuine compassion to ensure every patient receives personalized attention.

Thanh Pham, O.D.

Lead Optometrist

Dr. Thanh Pham has been practicing optometry since 1999. She was raised in Puyallup, WA. After graduating from Puyallup High School she attended the University of Washington to do her undergraduate studies. She later went on to complete her Doctor of Optometry at the University of California, Berkeley School of Optometry.

To further her optometric training, Dr. Pham has studied under multiple clinical settings. She has trained at the Bascom and Palmer Eye Institute (No. 1 Eye Hospital in the country) in Florida. She has trained at the Missouri Eye Institute, a referral based surgical center working with secondary and tertiary ophthalmologists in the treatment of ocular disease and co-management of refractive surgeries. She also completed ocular disease externships at Martinez VA Hospital, Oakland VA Hospital, and at the Northern Navajo Medical Center in Shiprock, New Mexico.

Certifications & Credentials

  • Certified by the National Board of Optometrists for the treatment and management of ocular disease
  • Certified by the Drug Enforcement Administration for controlled substances
  • Doctor of Optometry, UC Berkeley School of Optometry
  • Advanced training at Bascom Palmer Eye Institute, Missouri Eye Institute, and multiple VA Hospitals
