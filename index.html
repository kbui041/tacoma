Welcome to Tacoma Family Eyecare

Your trusted partner in maintaining optimal vision and eye health for your entire family.

At Tacoma Family Eyecare, we combine clinical excellence with compassionate care. Our experienced team of optometrists and support staff are dedicated to providing comprehensive eye care services using the latest diagnostic technology and evidence-based treatments.

Conveniently located inside Costco Tacoma, we make it easy to prioritize your family's vision health. Whether you need a routine eye exam, contact lens fitting, or management of eye disease, we're here to help you see your best.

Complete Eye Care Services

Comprehensive Eye Exams

Complete evaluation including eye alignment, pupil testing, visual fields, and screening for cataracts, glaucoma, and macular degeneration.

Contact Lens Fittings

Pediatric Eye Care

Retinal Photography

Dry Eye Analysis

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment options for dry eye symptoms and discomfort.

Surgery Co-Management
