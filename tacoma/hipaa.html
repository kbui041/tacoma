Your Privacy Rights

Under HIPAA, you have specific rights regarding your protected health information, including:

  • The right to access and review your medical records
  • The right to request corrections to your health information
  • The right to request confidential communications
  • The right to receive a copy of this privacy notice

For complete details about your rights and our practices, please review our full Notice of Privacy Practices linked above.
